2/13 (Wed) Update Content

New Hero Luna!
The vengeful half-dragon from Wintenberg, Luna, has arrived! Add her to your team and see the strength she possesses!


During the event period, players will have a higher chance to Summon Luna!
Don’t miss this chance to get the limited Hero Luna!
※ Luna’s Limited Drop Rate Up is available only on the Global and Asia servers.
※ During Luna’s Summon event, when using her banner to summon, the drop rate is increased to 1% and no other 5★ Heroes can be summoned.
※ Luna is a limited-time Hero and will not be added to the Covenant Summon after the event has ended.
※ This Hero may be sold in the same or similar form in the future.

■ Schedule
2/13 (Wed) after the maintenance~ 2/27 (Wed) 14:59 UTC

■ Servers running this event:
- Global + Asia server

■ Guaranteed Summon System Information
1) The Guaranteed Summon system will only be applied to this limited summon Drop Rate Up banner.
2) When players summon during the banner, the summon counter will decrease by 1 for each summon that does not yield the banner Hero (max 120 summons).
3) If the Hero has not been summoned after the listed number of summons (120), the next summon is 100% guaranteed to be the banner Hero.
4) Summoning the Hero in the banner will result in the guaranteed summon counter being reset to the original number.
